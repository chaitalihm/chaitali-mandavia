01
How much does AI actually cost to deploy?
I kept hearing wildly different numbers thrown around at work — no one seemed to agree on what enterprise AI actually costs once you factor in oversight, compute, and setup. So I built a calculator that lets you put in your own numbers and see where the break-even is. Turns out the answer is: faster than most people think.

What would it actually look like to deploy agentic AI at an investment firm?
I picked a concrete use case — a Dubai-based firm tracking global semiconductor markets — and tried to work through the whole thing: how you'd pitch it, what you'd ask the client before building anything, what the system actually looks like, and how you'd know six months later if it worked. An iMessage bot that actually does things for you
I wanted to see how far you could push a text-based AI assistant — not a new app, just iMessage.
